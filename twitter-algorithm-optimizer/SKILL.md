# Twitter Algorithm Optimizer Skill

Improve tweet performance by aligning content with the known mechanics of Twitter's open-source ranking algorithm.

## When to Activate

- Writing a tweet and wanting to maximize reach for a specific audience
- Auditing a content strategy for algorithmic fit
- Understanding why certain tweets underperform
- Building a niche authority content plan

## Four Ranking Models

| Model | What It Measures | Implication |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Real-graph** | Predicted engagement from your followers | Content must resonate with your existing audience first |
| **SimClusters** | Community resonance (topic clusters) | Niche-specific language gets amplified within that cluster |
| **TwHIN** | Content-user fit across the graph | Consistent topical focus builds long-term distribution |
| **Tweepcred** | Account credibility score | Quality interactions > volume; buying engagement backfires |

## Six Optimization Strategies

1. **Maximize follower engagement** — ask direct questions, reference shared context
2. **Leverage community detection** — use the vocabulary your niche uses; cluster membership matters
3. **Build topical authority** — consistent themes signal expertise to TwHIN
4. **Build credibility** — meaningful replies > mass following; quality > volume
5. **Trigger engagement signals** — design for replies (ask a question), shares (make it quotable), saves (make it reference-worthy)
6. **Avoid penalties** — inflammatory framing, engagement bait ("RT if you agree"), and off-brand pivots all reduce distribution

## Four-Step Optimization Process

1. **Identify core message** — what's the one thing you want people to take away?
2. **Map to algorithm** — which ranking model is most relevant to your goal?
3. **Optimize for signals** — rewrite to invite a specific engagement type
4. **Check negative factors** — does any phrase risk being read as bait or inflammatory?

## Before/After Example

**Before:** "Excited to share our new product launch!"  
**After:** "After 6 months of building, [Product] is live. Here's the one problem it solves that nobody else was tackling 🧵"

Why it works: narrative hook, niche relevance, thread signal (more engagement), curiosity gap.

## Key Principle

Authentic community engagement beats viral attempts. Track what resonates with *your* specific audience — not generic Twitter best practices.
