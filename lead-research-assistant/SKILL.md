# Lead Research Assistant Skill

Systematically research potential customers: profile the company, score fit against your ICP, identify decision-makers, and draft a personalized outreach strategy.

## When to Activate

- Building a list of qualified prospects from a target market
- Researching a specific company before a sales call
- Identifying the right contacts at a target account
- Preparing personalized outreach messages at scale

## Research Workflow

### 1. Company Profile
Gather:
- Business model, industry, size, revenue stage
- Recent news, funding rounds, product launches
- Tech stack (from job postings, BuiltWith, etc.)
- Key challenges suggested by their public presence

### 2. ICP Fit Score (1–10)
Score against your ideal customer profile:
- Industry match
- Company size / ARR range
- Tech stack compatibility
- Growth signals (hiring, funding, expansion)
- Pain points alignment

### 3. Decision-Maker Identification
Find on LinkedIn / company website:
- Economic buyer (approves budget)
- Champion (uses the product daily)
- Technical evaluator (reviews integrations)
- Blocker (legal, security, IT)

### 4. Outreach Strategy
For each contact:
- Personalized opening hook (specific to their role + recent company news)
- Relevant value prop mapped to their pain point
- Suggested call-to-action

## Output Format

```markdown
## [Company Name]
**ICP Score:** 8/10  
**Reason:** Series B SaaS, 150 employees, uses Salesforce, hiring SDRs

### Key Contacts
| Name | Role | LinkedIn | Hook |
|------|------|----------|------|
| Jane Smith | VP Sales | /in/jsmith | Congrats on Series B — saw you're scaling the SDR team |

### Recommended Approach
- Channel: LinkedIn DM → email sequence
- Message angle: pipeline visibility for growing sales teams
```
